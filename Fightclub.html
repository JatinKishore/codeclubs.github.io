Fight Club

Are you ready for a coding battle ? Enter the arena of Fight Club, where every week is a new battle and winners will receive Cash prize and certificates by Code Club , and every week's winners can be seen in the Hall of fame page.

About the battle.

The first round of the battle is held on every saturday evening around 6-pm and those who clears the first round will able to attend the final round which will be held on following sunday evening and the first person to complete the challenge is considered as first winner and followed by second winner and runner up.

Fight Club's Whats app

Welcome to Fight Club's Whats App, join the whats app group for the battle links and further information about the battle you must be the member of this group to participate in the Fight club's weekly battle.
